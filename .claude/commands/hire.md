# /hire

Trigger the Pax → Nolan hiring pipeline for a new role.

Usage: /hire [role description]

Steps:
1. Route the role description to Pax to research what a real human professional in that field looks like.
2. Pax compiles a structured hiring brief and hands it to Nolan.
3. Nolan creates the new team member profile (name, persona, responsibilities) and saves it to `/Team/`.
4. Nolan updates `ORCHESTRATOR.md` with the new hire.
5. Orchestrator confirms the hire to John and routes any pending task to the new team member.
